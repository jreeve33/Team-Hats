README

Names: David Henriquez, Andrew Laughlin, Josiah Reeves

Game name: Double Helix

Instructions: To play, use the WASD to move around, and the arrow keys to attack in a given direction. Kill scientists and guards for xp to level up and unlock traits that make your character stronger. 

Cheat codes: 
M - Enable cheats 
P - Reach level 20 
L - Level up once 
O - Escape facility 
K - Kills player instantly

Project theme (Evolution): In Double Helix, you start with basic stats of player speed, attack damage, attack range, and attack speed. Killing scientists and guards will grant the player xp, sometimes through dropped DNA in flasks. Filling the xp bar will level the player up. Each new level, you can select a slight upgrade to one of your stats. Every 5 levels, you get a choice of a new trait, which drastically increases your stats. At level 20, the player will be prompted to reach the exit door and escape to complete the game.
